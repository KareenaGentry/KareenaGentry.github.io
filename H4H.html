Homes for Haiti Mission

Homes for Haiti follows Christ’s calling to "heal the afflicted and care for the poor" by turning trash into housing. By growing self-worth by offering employment and transforming whole villages from just a few hospitable homes, Homes for Haiti calls people to become their full selves by living into who God has called them to be. By this act of love, the people live in a rich relationship with the father and thriving communities are created.

“My people will live in peaceful dwelling places,
in secure homes, in undisturbed places of rest.”
-Isaiah 32:18



Our First Project!

Unlocking Eden's first ministry is called Homes for Haiti. It is a for-profit business, locally run and employed. The ministry will be a franchise with the Mexico-based company EcoDom (learn more here). The homes are built out of recycled material with over 2 tons of plastic used per home. This business model provides employment, it is environmentally friendly and focused, and makes something beautiful out of something initially seen as ugly.

Fun Facts!

  • The homes are secure and earthquake resistant.
  • It only takes 7 days to build.
  • Every home includes a living room, 2 bedrooms, a kitchen, and a bathroom.
  • Each house is made of 2 tons recycled plastic.
